  • About Bachelor of Science

    B.Sc. or Bachelor of Science is an undergraduate degree of three years duration which is universally offered across institutes and universities across India. B. Sc. is one of the most popular courses opted for by students who have a flair for scientific aptitude and zeal for research-oriented and calculative approaches based on a proven systematic method. Science is all about experimentation, research and discovery. A BSc degree is a culmination of both theoretical and practical ways of learning. Pursuing a B.Sc. degree also opens up multiple avenues for a student and he/she can either opt for higher studies in Science or any other discipline which may be related to or different from the subjects studied during graduation. However, a Master of Science (MSc) is the most popular higher education alternative opted for by students after completing BSc.

  • What are some of the popular BSc courses that can be pursued after Class 12?

    There are several popular science and course choices after Class 12. BE/BTech, MBBS, Bachelor of Architecture (BArch), BSc, Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), BSc (IT & Software), Post Basic Nursing, Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharma) etc. are some popular science options after Class 12.

  • Is BSc better than BTech?

    BTech (Bachelor of Technology) is usually considered better as compared to BSc as it is a 4 years technical professional course, which has good career opportunities.

  • Which BSc course is best?

    There are a variety of BSc courses offered by different colleges in India. The best BSc course depends on one’s interest area. However, here is a list of some popular BSc courses that offer good job opportunities after graduation: BSc Computer Science, BSc Mathematics, BSc Agriculture, BSc Horticulture, BVSc (Veterinary Science), BSc Forestry, BSc Biotechnology, BFSc (Fisheries Science), BSc Nursing, BSc Nautical Science, BSc Physics
