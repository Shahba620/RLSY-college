M.TECH

  • About Master of Technology

    M.tech is a professional postgraduate engineering master degree programme awarded to candidates after completion of two years of study in the discipline of engineering. This degree is predicated in a specific branch of engineering. In India, the ME/MTech degree is offered across various specializations. These specializations include Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Electrical Engineering, VLSI, Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering etc. This degree course is offered under the following categories:
    1. Regular (Full-Time)
    2. Sponsored (Full-Time)
    3. Part Time Mtech
    Project Staff (For those who are presently working on sponsored projects) This master degree programme is also receptive to working diploma holders or diploma holders having work experience for specified period.

  • What are the different job profiles offered to a MTech graduate?

    After completing ME is Master of Engineering and that of MTech is Master of Technology, a candidate can easily find a job in research and development companies, manufacturing firms, and IT companies as Project Manager, Research Associate, and Senior Engineers. Some of the candidates also prefer academic jobs such as teaching in a college or becoming a professor.

  • What is the scope of MTech in information technology?

    In today's career opportunities, IT Specialists with higher qualifications are required in almost all range of employment fields such as finance, arts, medicine, defense, and other areas. Some of the leading IT firms also prefer candidates with MTech degree.

  • What is the average salary after MTech in India?

    After getting a MTech degree, a candidate can can earn an average of 3- 10 lakhs annually. The salary of a candidate also depends on the profile he/she is working on.

  • What is the full form of MTech. What is the duration of the course?

    MTech stands for Masters of technology. It is an postgraduate engineering degree awarded to a candidate. The duration of this degree course is two years.
